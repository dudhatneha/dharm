Waste Tracker Application

Waste Tracker application has been designed and developed to showcase life cycle of a plastic waste right from collection by a Swachhta Grahi to recycling in an environmentally responsible manner. It will help in increasing traceability and transparency and trackability in collection of plastic waste. It connects all stakeholders involved in plastic waste collection, including Rag Pickers, Kabadiwala, Drivers, Recyclers & PIBOs. It analyses the volume and cost of the collected waste. It tracks down all the waste shipments and transit of the same to a recycling facility through Geo-Tagging Facility.

The Waste Tracker application is an innovative solution developed to address the critical issue of plastic waste management. Designed to showcase the complete life cycle of plastic waste, from collection to recycling, this app offers a range of features that enhance traceability, transparency, and trackability in the collection and recycling of plastic waste. By connecting all stakeholders involved in the process, including Swachhta Gratis (cleanliness workers), Rag Pickers, Kabadiwalas (scrap dealers), Drivers, Recyclers, and Producers, Importers, and Brand owners (PIBOs), the Waste Tracker app revolutionises the way plastic waste is managed..

One of the primary advantages of the Waste Tracker app is its ability to increase traceability and transparency in plastic waste collection. By utilising the app, Swachhta Grahis, Rag Pickers, and Kabadiwalas can easily record and track the collection of plastic waste. The app allows them to input data regarding the quantity of waste collected, which provides valuable insights into waste generation patterns. This information can be used to identify areas of improvement, optimise waste collection strategies, and ensure efficient resource allocation.

Moreover, the Waste Tracker app offers features for analysing the volume and cost of the collected waste. By recording the quantity of plastic waste collected and associated costs, users can gain a better understanding of the financial implications and overall efficiency of waste collection operations. This data-driven analysis can assist in the development of more effective waste management policies and practices, leading to optimised resource allocation and reduced waste generation.

The app's tracking capabilities are another key feature that sets it apart. Through the Geo-Tagging facility, the Waste Tracker app tracks the entire journey of waste shipments from collection points to recycling facilities. This technology records the locations of waste shipments, providing real-time updates on their transit. By leveraging this feature, stakeholders can ensure the responsible transportation of waste and monitor its progress throughout the recycling process. Additionally, the Geo-Tagging facility acts as a safeguard against illegal disposal or diversion of waste, promoting environmental responsibility and compliance with waste management regulations.

The Waste Tracker app offers several advantages for the different stakeholders involved in plastic waste management. For Swachhta Grahis, the app provides a streamlined platform for recording and managing their collection activities. They can easily log the quantity of waste collected, generate reports for accountability purposes, and efficiently communicate with other stakeholders. This transparency and efficiency contribute to a more organised and effective waste collection process.

Similarly, Kabadiwalas benefit from the Waste Tracker app by gaining access to a marketplace feature. This feature connects them with potential buyers, expanding their market reach and improving income-generating opportunities. The app serves as a platform for Kabadiwalas to showcase the types and quantities of waste they have available, streamlining the buying and selling process. This feature not only benefits Kabadiwalas but also promotes a circular economy by facilitating the recycling and reuse of plastic waste.

Recyclers also find value in the Waste Tracker app, as it offers a centralised platform for receiving and processing waste shipments. By tracking the incoming waste, managing inventory, and planning recycling activities efficiently, recyclers can optimise their operations. The app also assists in compliance with regulatory requirements by providing a digital record of waste transactions and recycling processes, facilitating audits and regulatory reporting.

In conclusion, the Waste Tracker application is a powerful tool that addresses the complex challenges of plastic waste management. By increasing traceability, transparency, and trackability, it revolutionises the way plastic waste is collected and recycled. The app's ability to connect all stakeholders involved in the process, analyse volume and cost, and track waste shipments through the Geo-Tagging facility brings numerous advantages. These include optimised resource allocation, improved efficiency, reduced environmental impact, enhanced market access, and compliance with waste management regulations. The Waste Tracker app plays a vital role in promoting responsible waste management practices.
